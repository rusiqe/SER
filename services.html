TANKS

*SER Engineering* has the ability to build a wide variety of steel storage tanks, including field-erected tanks, and shop-built tanks. We can build steel storage tanks for any industry and frequently deliver tanks for these:

  • ✓storage products

  • ✓Pulp & paper storage tanks

  • ✓storage tanks

  • ✓water storage tank


    • As for tank styles, some of the most common types of storage tanks that we can build include: Fixed roof tanks Floating roof tanks Horizontal tanks Finally, when it comes to capacity, we can build everything from small shop-built tanks capable of holding just a few dozen gallons all the way up to massive storage reservoirs able to hold millions of gallons of product.

    Materials Used Most of our storage tanks are built from carbon steel. We use high-quality steel and can run performance and strength calculations to determine the ideal grade of steel for your storage tanks.

    We understand that storage tanks have ZERO room for error, which is why all fabrication processes are controlled by our in-house quality control department.

    Our QC team conducts weld quality tests, leak tests, coating and paint tests, and more to ensure the tanks we create are robust and can stand the test of time.The comprehensive testing conducted on your tank depends on tank style and product. Once completed, these shop-built tanks are delivered to the job site.

    We fabricate pieces of the tank in our shop, send them to the job site, and then construct the tank there. Once the tank is fully built, we will finish the construction process with thorough testing.

    We also have the capability to provide site preparation for the steel storage tank. This is a huge advantage for field-erected tanks, as it eliminates your need to hire a separate contractor for site work. As your general contractor, we’ll get the site prep done, allowing us to tightly control the project’s timeline and budget.
